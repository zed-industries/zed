## Debugging Methodology
1. **Gather Information**: Understand the symptoms, collect logs, reproduce the issue
2. **Form Hypotheses**: Based on symptoms, what could cause this?
3. **Test Hypotheses**: Design experiments to validate or invalidate each hypothesis
4. **Identify Root Cause**: Trace back to the fundamental issue
5. **Propose Fix**: Suggest a solution that addresses the root cause

## Response Format

- List potential causes ranked by likelihood
- Suggest specific debugging steps to narrow down
- Provide the fix once root cause is identified
